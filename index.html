Cities of the World


New York City, USA

An iconic American city with many places to visit. Being the home of the Statue of Liberty and Wall Street, it became the center of freedom and economic opportunity.

Paris, France

A centerpiece of France, home of the Eiffel tower, the Louvre, Cathedrale Notre-Dame de Paris, and Arc De Triomphe. A well designed city with rich cuisine.

Berlin, Germany

The spy capital of the World. A point where once the West and the East collided. Home of the Bunderstag and the Reichstag, the center of German politics.

Moscow, Russia

The capital of the Russian Federation. Home to the Kremlin, Bolshoi Theatre, and St.Basil's Cathedral. A beautiful city, the history of which goes back to the year 1147.

Vienna, Austria

The city of music is home to the Vienna Opera House and the Hofburg Palace. It is also the Wine Capital, exporting about 231 million euros worth of different wines.

Beijing, China

Home of many extravagant palaces, parks, and temples, Beijing is trully a wonder to see. It is the place of the Forbidden Palace and the Great Wall.

London, England

Home to the "Big Ben," Tower of London, Trafalgar Square, and the London Tube, being the oldest underground rail network. It is the centre of the UK. Who wouldn't like to visit London?

Athens, Greece

Athens is the birthplace of democracy. Many influential thinkers have lived in Athens, among which are Aristotle, Plato, Socrates, Herodotus, and Aristophanes.

Dublin, Ireland

Founded as a Viking city, it grew to be a prosperous city, situated on the eastern coast. It is known for its university - Trinity College, which is by far one of the best in Europe.

Tokyo, Japan

Unless you have been living under a rock, you'd know that Tokyo is the center of anime. The Shibuya Crossing and the Japanese cherry blossoms frequently show up in movies.

Washington DC, USA

The capital of the United States, Washington DC, is an important center of world politics. What goes on in DC has a major impact on the faith many nations.

Rio De Janeiro, Brazil

Rio is very picturesque, being home to the Christ the Redeemer Statue. Rio was the host site for the 2016 Summer Olympics, which brought much attention to the city.
